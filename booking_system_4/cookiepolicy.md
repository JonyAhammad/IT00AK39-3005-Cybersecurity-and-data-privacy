# Cookie Policy – Booking System

## 1. What Are Cookies
Cookies are small files stored in the  browser. They help the Booking System work correctly and securely.

## 2. Cookies We Use
This system uses only essential cookies:
- **Session cookie** – keeps users logged in
- **CSRF token** – protects the system from fake or malicious requests

These cookies do not store passwords or sensitive personal data.

## 3. Why We Use Cookies
Cookies are used to:
- Manage user login sessions
- Protect forms and actions from security attacks
- Ensure the system works correctly

## 4. Consent
By using the Booking System and logging in, you agree to the use of essential cookies. These cookies are required for the system to function.

## 5. Third-Party Cookies
The Booking System does not use third-party or tracking cookies.

## 6. Managing Cookies
Users can block cookies in their browser, but this may prevent the system from working properly.

## 7. Changes
This Cookie Policy may be updated if the system changes.

## 8. Contact
For questions about cookies or privacy, users can contact the system administrator.
